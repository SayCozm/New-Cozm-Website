The Case for Change

Why Now?

Five converging forces have made manual global mobility processes not just inefficient — but a genuine liability. The window to act is open. It won't be open forever.

Key Trends for 2025

Three Forces Reshaping Global Mobility

⚖️

Intensifying Compliance Demands

Changing immigration rules (US, UK), ETA & ETIAS, EU Posted Worker Directives, Social Security A1 obligations, tax legislation changes, EU Labour Authority inspections — all accelerating simultaneously.

💡

Accessible, Tailored Technology

More affordable, organisation-specific platforms are enabling smaller teams to adopt automation that was once only available to enterprise budgets — levelling the playing field.

🤖

AI at the Heart of Operations

AI reduces reliance on service providers, minimises email overload, increases efficiency in case management, and frees specialists for talent strategy. The tools are ready. Incumbents aren't.

The Full Picture

Five Reasons to Act Now

01

Regulatory Complexity Is Accelerating

UK ETA, Germany A1 requirements, EU salary transparency rules — all launched in the past 18 months. Manual processes from 2020 are now a compliance liability, not just an inefficiency.

UK ETA EU ETIAS Germany A1 EU Salary Transparency EU Posted Worker Directive
02

Budget Pressure Isn't Going Away

CFOs are scrutinising mobility costs while assignment volumes grow. The average cost of a single A1 filing through traditional providers is €150–400. Multiply that across your programme. The Cozm reduces total mobility spend by 40–60%.

03

Your Team Should Drive Strategy, Not Paperwork

60–70% of global mobility team time is currently spent on repetitive administration. That's expertise wasted on tasks a machine can do in seconds. The Cozm gives back 15–20 hours per week to every team — redirecting talent toward strategic priorities.

04

Bad Data Is a Compliance Crisis Waiting to Happen

Scattered data across portals creates compliance gaps, missed renewals, and the inability to forecast. Without a single source of truth, every audit is a risk. The Cozm provides real-time dashboards, audit-ready reporting, and a unified data layer across your entire programme.

05

AI Has Finally Caught Up — And Incumbents Can't Follow

Since 2024, AI has reached the capability threshold for global mobility: auto-classifying assignments, pre-populating forms, predicting compliance risks, and monitoring 28 countries' regulations in real-time. Legacy platforms built in the 2010s cannot redesign their architecture without breaking existing customers. This is a unique window — and it won't stay open.

"The future is AI-led. Incumbents can't adapt fast enough — creating a unique window for agile, tech-native solutions like The Cozm."
