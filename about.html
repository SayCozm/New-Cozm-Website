About Us

We Are The Cozm

Built not to digitise the status quo — but to completely rethink it. From one vision to a globally distributed team spanning five continents.

Our Story

From Frustration to Foundation

The name The Cozm comes from the Greek word Cosmos — meaning a world without boundaries. That name says everything about what we're building.

Our founder, Benjamin Oghene, held senior roles at three of the Big Four, most recently as Global Head of Technology and Innovation at PwC. He saw first-hand how outdated, manual processes were slowing down how organisations managed mobile workforces — siloed systems, reactive compliance, and tools that didn't reflect how people actually work today.

With a background in computer science, Ben set out to build something better. The Cozm began not to digitise the status quo, but to completely rethink it.

Whether you are deploying one person or one hundred, you'll do it with fewer delays, lower costs, and complete confidence at every step.

Benjamin Oghene
Founder & CEO

Former Global Head of Technology & Innovation, PwC. Big Four veteran. Computer science background. Builder of the world's first Digital Global Mobility Workforce.

Build a world where skilled individuals can work anywhere regardless of personal demographics. Automated global mobility solutions mitigate human cognitive biases — creating a more reliable, fair, and equitable system. Not just for business, but for the greater good.
